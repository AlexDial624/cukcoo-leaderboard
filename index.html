How This Works

Presence Time

Your presence time is the total time you've spent in the Cuckoo room. Presence is detected approximately every 5 minutes via snapshots.

  • When you appear in a snapshot, we assume you joined right after the previous snapshot (generous estimate).
  • When you disappear from a snapshot, we assume you left just before it.
  • If there's a gap of more than 30 minutes between snapshots, we cap assumed presence to prevent inflated numbers.
  • If the activity feed shows you "joined", we use that precise timestamp instead.

Pomodoro Count

Your pomodoro count is the number of work sessions you participated in. You get credit if you were present when the timer started OR joined within 5 minutes of it starting.

  • Everyone in the room during a pomodoro gets +1 (not just who clicked start).
  • 5-minute grace period: if you join slightly late, you still count.
  • If you leave before the timer ends, you still get the count (you were there for the start).

Work Time

Your work time is the actual overlap between your presence and running work timers.

  • If a 25-minute timer runs and you're present for all 25 minutes, you get 25 minutes of work time.
  • If you leave after 15 minutes, you only get 15 minutes of work time.
  • If you join 10 minutes into a timer and stay until the end, you get 15 minutes.

Break Count & Break Time

Your break count and break time work the same way as pomodoros and work time, but for break timers.

  • +1 break count if present at start or joined within 5 minutes.
  • Break time = actual overlap between your presence and the break timer.

Currently Online

The "Currently in Room" section shows who was in the Cuckoo room at the most recent snapshot. Updates approximately every 5 minutes.

Ranking

Users are ranked by total presence time (time spent in the room), from highest to lowest.

How This Works

Presence Time

Your presence time is the total time you've spent in the Cuckoo room. You are counted as "present" from the moment you appear in the room until you leave. Presence is detected approximately every 5 minutes.

  • When you join the room, your presence timer starts.
  • When you leave the room (close the tab, navigate away, etc.), your presence timer stops.
  • If you leave and rejoin, each session is tracked separately and added to your total.

Pomodoro Count

Your pomodoro count is the number of work sessions you have started. A pomodoro is counted when you click to start a work timer (e.g., "25 minute work session").

  • Each time you start a work timer, your pomodoro count increases by 1.
  • Only the person who clicks "start" gets credit for that pomodoro.
  • If you stop/skip a timer early, the pomodoro still counts (you started it).

Work Time

Your work time is the sum of all work timer durations you have started.

  • If you start a 25-minute work session, 25 minutes is added to your work time.
  • If you start a 50-minute work session, 50 minutes is added.
  • This measures intended work time based on timers started, not actual elapsed time.

Break Count & Break Time

Your break count and break time work the same way as pomodoros and work time, but for break timers.

  • Each break timer you start adds 1 to your break count.
  • The duration of that break timer is added to your break time.

Currently Online

The "Currently in Room" section shows who is in the Cuckoo room right now. This is detected in real-time and updates approximately every 5 minutes.

Ranking

Users are ranked by total presence time (time spent in the room), from highest to lowest.
